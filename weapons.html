Weapons

Food and drink aren’t the only things that will help you survive in the PIB. Weapons are vital for survival in the PIB, if you don’t have one: get one.

  • Weapons break, they always do:
  • Novice level: Break after 2 missions
  • Intermediate level: Break after 5 missions
  • Expert level: Break after 10 missions
